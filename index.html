The Heart

A poem by Denzel to Azure

The heart The center the main component to the human body
Without it we cannot live and with it its used to feel
People can disrespect it take advantage of and shatter it completely
But it can also be healed mended and raised to a tier no one else can reach
Giving your heart to someone is not an easy task
But this Valentine's I will make it special,

Will you be my valentine?
